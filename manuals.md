## User Manual: Mobile App

### Getting Started

#### 1. Register & Login
- **Sign up** with email and password or use Google
- Choose your account type: **Oyente** (Listener) or **Artista** (Artist)
- Complete the onboarding process to set your favorite genres and artists

#### 2. Onboarding
After registration, you'll be guided through:
- **Genre selection**: Pick your favorite music genres
- **Artist discovery**: Follow artists you love

### Main Features

#### Home
- **Your shortcuts**: Quick access to Liked Songs and your most played content (playlists, albums, artists)
- **New releases from artists you follow**: Latest singles, EPs and albums from followed artists
- **Recently played**: Your recent listening history
- **Discover more from {Artist}**: Random artist you follow with related playlists/albums

#### Search
- Unified search across songs, albums, playlists, and artists
- Filter by type (All, Songs, Albums, Artists, Playlists, Profiles)
- Top result for best matches

#### Library
- **Playlists**: Create and manage your own playlists
- **Liked Songs**: All your favorite tracks in one place
- **History**: See what you've been listening to

#### Player
- Full-featured music player with:
  - Play, pause, skip (next/previous), shuffle, repeat modes
  - Seek/scrub through timeline
  - Queue management (view, reorder and clear queue)
  - Video playback for music videos (when available)
- Mini player for navigation while browsing
- Like/unlike songs directly from the player

#### Profile
- View and edit your profile information
- View other users' profiles
- Follow/unfollow other users and artists
- See followers and following lists

### Features for Artists

#### Artist Profile Management
- Upload profile picture and banner
- Edit bio and add social media links
- Manage photo carousel (up to 5 images)
- Set up Artist Pick (featured content: album/EP/single/playlist)

#### Publishing Releases
- Upload singles, EPs, and albums
- Add metadata: title, genres, collaborators (credits), cover art, audio files
- Schedule releases or publish immediately
- Set regional availability

#### Discography
Your artist profile automatically organizes:
- **Popular**: Your top 5 most played tracks
- **Albums**: All your albums sorted by release date
- **Singles & EPs**: All your singles and EPs sorted by release date
- **Appears On**: Collaborations in other albums and public playlists that include your songs

### Social Features

#### Following
- Follow other users and artists
- See followers and following lists
- View friends' activity: recent plays, new playlists, shared content
- Activity feed with filters (by type: plays, playlists, shares)

#### Sharing
- Share songs and playlists with other Melody users
- Share externally via link to social media and messaging apps

### Notifications
- New releases from artists you follow
- Activity from users you follow: new public playlists, shared content
- Deep linking: tap notifications to jump directly to the content

### Settings & Preferences
- Manage notification preferences (enable/disable by type)
- Content filters (explicit content on/off)
- Privacy settings for profile and playlists (public/private)
